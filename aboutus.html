ABOUT US



WatcHGoods is a store to the greatest collection of used luxury watches. All the watches being sold at WatcHGoods are certified as authentic and Collector Quality whereas there is no need to think twice or hesitate about the watch's quality that you're going to buy.



WatcHGoods has several offline stores that are placed at USA and Asia. Originally, WatcHGoods is located at Jakarta, Indonesia. With the growth of the demands of the customers outside Jakarta, WatcHGoods finally expanded to other regions and countries such as New York, Vietnam, etc. WatcHGoods is also available online for buyers that can't reach the offline store at the moment. No need to hesitate about buying the watches online because WatcHGoods will always have a quality check after you buy the watch and will also maintain the quality stay at it's finest until it reaches your hands.
